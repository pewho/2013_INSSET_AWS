Culture

Relationships Engage early, engage often Bust Silos TM Be open Stop finger pointing Eradicate “last-mile syndrome” Communication Talk is cheap (get out of your chair) Involve each other in core processes and decisions Ask Questions Don’t say “no” Invite everyone to stand-ups and retrospectives
